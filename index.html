About Me

I am Naseeb Ahammed from the southern part of India called Kerala. It has nearly 600 km of Arabian Sea shoreline and India's tropical Malabar Coast. Because of our natural beauty, it is easy to be creative. When I was a kid, my dream used to be a Software Engineer, unfortunately, I couldn't. So I decided to learn Web Designing.

In the beginning, I went to an institution to learn Web Design. While learning, I was using the Internet to learn and improve my knowledge in a short period. That was the movement I realized, the syllabus I was learning is the old traditional method. The world is changing and technology is changing day by day, so I decided to self-learn everything from the internet.

From that day onwards, I am still learning new things on my own, it made me confident. And on my 20th birthday, I became a Web Design teacher in an institution. That was a big step in my life. After some time I resigned and became a Freelance Designer.
